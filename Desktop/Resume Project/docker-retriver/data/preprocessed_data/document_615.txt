Article 243A: Gram Sabha - Constitution of India Part IX Article 243A Gram Sabha A Gram Sabha may exercise such powers and perform such functions at the village level as the Legislature of a State may, by law, provide. Summary This Article in its present form was not debated in the Constituent Assembly. It was inserted by the Constitution (Seventy-third Amendment) Act, 1992 and the Original Part IX was omitted by the Constitution (Seventh Amendment) Act, 1956.
