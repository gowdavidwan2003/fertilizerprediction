Part IX Archives - Constitution of India Part IX The Panchayats Part IX of the Constitution was inserted by the Constitution (Seventy-third Amendment) Act, 1992. It contains provisions for local self government at the rural level. The original Part IX was omitted by the Constitution (Seventh Amendment) Act, 1956.
