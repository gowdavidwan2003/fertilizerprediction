About ConQuest ConQuest is India’s premiere national quiz on the Indian Constitution, History and Politics, and CLPR’s annual flagship event. ConQuest encourages undergraduate and graduate students from the natural sciences, engineering, social sciences, humanities, and law to engage with the Indian Constitution, law, politics, history and current affairs in a fun yet rigorous way. ConQuest 2019 was open for undergraduate and graduate University students. It brought about two key changes to the format of the Quiz. First, the number of members in each team was increased to three. Second, to promote gender diversity and inclusivity each team was required to have atleast one non-male member. Read more here.
