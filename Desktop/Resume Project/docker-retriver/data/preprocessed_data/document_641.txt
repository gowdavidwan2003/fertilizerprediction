Article 243Z: Audit of accounts of Municipalities - Constitution of India Part IXA Article 243Z Audit of accounts of Municipalities The Legislature of a State may, by law, make provisions with respect to the maintenance of accounts by the Municipalities and the auditing of such accounts. Summary This Article in its present form was not debated in the Constituent Assembly. It was inserted by the Constitution (Seventy-fourth Amendment) Act, 1992.
