Article 268A: Service tax levied by Union and collected and appropriated by the Union and the States - Constitution of India Part XII Article 268A Service tax levied by Union and collected and appropriated by the Union and the States Omitted by the Constitution (One Hundred and First Amendment) Act, 2016, s. 7 (w.e.f. 16-9-2016). SUMMARY Article 268A was only inserted in 2003 through the Constitution (Eighty-eighth Amendment) Act, 2003 . This Article was subsequently ommitted in 2016 through the Constitution (One Hundred and First Amendment) Act, 2016.
