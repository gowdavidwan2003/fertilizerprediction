Article 383: Provision as to Governors of Provinces (Omitted) - Constitution of India Part XXI Article 383 Provision as to Governors of Provinces Omitted by the Constitution (Seventh Amendment) Act, 1956, s. 29 and Sch. (w.e.f. 1-11-1956). Version 1 Article 383, Constitution of India 1950 Any person holding office as Governor in any Province immediately before the commencement of this Constitution shall on such commencement be the Governor of the corresponding State specified in Part A of the First Schedule until a new Governor has been appointed in accordance with the provisions of Chapter II of Part VI and has entered upon his office.
