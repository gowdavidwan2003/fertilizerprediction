Article 243ZA: Elections to the Municipalities - Constitution of India Part IXA Article 243ZA Elections to the Municipalities (1) The superintendence, direction and control of the preparation of electoral rolls for, and the conduct of, all elections to the Municipalities shall be vested in the State Election Commission referred to in article 243K. (2) Subject to the provisions of this Constitution, the Legislature of a State may, by law, make provision with respect to all matters relating to, or in connection with, elections to the Municipalities. Summary This Article in its present form was not debated in the Constituent Assembly. It was inserted by the Constitution (Seventy-fourth Amendment) Act, 1992.
