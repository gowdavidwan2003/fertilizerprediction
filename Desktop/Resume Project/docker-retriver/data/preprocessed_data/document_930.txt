Mehta was one among the 15 women members of the Constituent Assembly and often a strong voice on issues affecting women. The ceremonial presentation of the Flag took place on the on the momentous occasion of India’s first Independence Day. It followed the pledge taking of the Constituent Assembly led by its Chairman, Rajendra Prasad and the formal declaration about the Assembly’s assumption of power over the country’s governance .
