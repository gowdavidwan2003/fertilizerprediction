(2) Any such law as is referred to in clause (1) shall not be deemed to be an amendment of this Constitution for the purposes of article 368 notwithstanding that it contains any provision which amends or has the effect of amending this Constitution. Summary This provision was not part of the Constitution of India, 1950. It was inserted by the Constitution (Fourteenth Amendment) Act, 1962.
