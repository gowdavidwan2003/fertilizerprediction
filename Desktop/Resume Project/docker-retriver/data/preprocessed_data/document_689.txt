Article 371-I: Special provision with respect to the State of Goa - Constitution of India Part XXI Article 371-I Special provision with respect to the State of Goa Notwithstanding anything in this Constitution, the Legislative Assembly of the State of Goa shall consist of not less than thirty members. Summary Article 371-I was not a part of the Constitution of India 1950. It was inserted by the Constitution (Fifty-sixth Amendment) Act, 1987, following the creation of the state of Goa.
