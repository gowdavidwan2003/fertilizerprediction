Article 300A: Persons not to be deprived of property save by authority of law - Constitution of India Part XII Article 300A Persons not to be deprived of property save by authority of law No person shall be deprived of his property save by authority of law. Summary This Article was introduced in the Constitution in 1978 through the Constitution (Forty-fourth Amendment) Act, 1978 and therefore was not discussed in the Constituent Assembly.
