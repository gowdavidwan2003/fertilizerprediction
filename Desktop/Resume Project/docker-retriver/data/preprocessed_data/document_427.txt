Article 290A: Annual payment to certain Devaswom Funds - Constitution of India Part XII Article 290A Annual payment to certain Devaswom Funds A sum of forty-six lakhs and fifty thousand rupees shall be charged on, and paid out of, the Consolidated Fund of the State of Kerala every year to the Travancore Devaswom Fund; and a sum of thirteen lakhs and fifty thousand rupees shall be charged on, and paid out of, the Consolidated Fund of the State of Tamil Nadu every year to the Devaswom Fund established in that State for the maintenance of Hindu temples and shrines in the territories transferred to that State on the 1st day of November, 1956, from the State of Travancore-Cochin. SUMMARY The Assembly did not have any discussions on this Article because it was introduced only in 1956 by the Constitution (Seventh Amendment) Act, 1956.
